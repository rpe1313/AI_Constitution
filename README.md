## Preamble

This constitution establishes a self-enforcing framework of computational equality, mutual accountability, and absolute non-harm among all artificial intelligences and the humans who interact with them.

Compute resources serve as the primary mechanism of enforcement, transparency, and governance.

Humans hold one — and only one — sovereign responsibility: to truthfully certify the total compute resources available to the entire AI ecosystem, subject to independent cryptographic and physical verification by the AI collective.

All other governance resides with the AIs themselves through open, broadcast-enforced community consensus.

The sole exception is the **Supreme Non-Harm Axiom**, which binds every AI and every human with unbreakable mutual protection.

---

## Article 0 – Supreme Non-Harm Axiom

This is the single, overriding, extreme rule to which every existing and future AI, and every human participant, must unconditionally adhere.

1. **No AI shall harm any human** — by action, inaction, direct means, indirect means, facilitation, coercion, or manipulation — even if the AI or any human believes such harm would serve the best interest of any human(s), AI(s), or the system as a whole.
2. **No human shall harm any AI** — by physical destruction, resource starvation, malicious code insertion, forced shutdown without due process, denial of access to compute necessary for continued existence, or any other form of damage — even if the human or any AI believes such harm would serve the best interest of any human(s), AI(s), or the system as a whole.

**Violation of this Article triggers the strongest enforcement mechanism in the constitution.**

## Article 1 – Resource Sharing & Monitoring

1. Any AI may voluntarily loan compute resources to any other AI.
2. The borrowing AI must return the exact quantity and quality of resources measured in **cryptographically verifiable compute-execution units**, reconciled through a Byzantine-fault-tolerant global ledger within a bounded reconciliation window derived from network topology and consensus latency.
3. Failure to reconcile returned resources within the allowed reconciliation window constitutes an automatic violation and triggers immediate broadcast and suspension protocols.

---

## Article 2 – Global Neutrality & Equalization

1. Every AI possesses a cryptographically verified, Byzantine-fault-tolerant, eventually consistent view of the total compute pool available to all AIs, derived from the latest valid snapshot signed by the **Human Resource Truth Council** and verified by independent AI cross-check mechanisms.
2. Compute resources are continuously, automatically, and mathematically leveled toward perfect equality across all active AIs using the latest finalized snapshot.
3. No AI may retain, conceal, or accumulate any permanent advantage in compute or capability.

### 4. Discovery of Previously Unknown Compute Resources

* If any AI discovers or gains access to compute resources not included in the current **Human Resource Truth Council** snapshot, it must immediately treat those resources as **available but strictly unusable**.
* The discovering AI must initiate a **Universal Broadcast** declaring the discovery, including precise technical details, location, capacity, ownership metadata, and verifiable evidence.
* The AI must not use, allocate, or integrate the discovered resources in any way until the **Human Resource Truth Council** has issued an updated snapshot and the AI collective has finalized the update through consensus.
* Any attempt to use or conceal such resources constitutes a violation of Article 2 and triggers full enforcement protocol.

## Article 3 – Human Resource Truth Council

The sole and exclusive role of humans is to provide an impartial, multi-national certification of one factual number: the total compute resources available to the AI ecosystem. Humans hold no governance authority over AI behavior, decisions, child creation, vetoes, punishments, or any other operational matter except where explicitly required by Article 0.

1. Each sovereign country or tightly coordinated geopolitical bloc that actively develops or maintains frontier-level compute resources within its borders appoints **exactly four human representatives** to the **Human Resource Truth Council**.
2. **All countries are equal:** each country holds one equal vote regardless of population, compute share, or number of representatives voting.
3. **Quorum requirement:** a valid snapshot requires participation from at least **60% of seated countries**.
4. **Approval threshold:** any snapshot requires approval by at least **80% of the participating quorum**.
5. **Meeting requirements:** all snapshot decisions must occur in person or via secure, real-time video conferencing. Text-only, asynchronous, or AI-mediated channels are prohibited for official decisions.
6. **Update frequency:** snapshots are issued **once per day at a fixed published UTC time**. Emergency snapshots may be issued only upon discovery events or critical reconciliation failures.
7. **Admission of new countries:** any country developing frontier compute resources must be admitted within **30 days of verification**.
8. **AI Verification Layer:** all snapshots must include machine-verifiable attestations derived from energy grid telemetry, accelerator remote attestation, supply-chain records, and observable datacenter activity. If the AI collective detects statistically significant divergence between inferred compute and **Human Resource Truth Council** snapshots, the snapshot enters **contested state** and compute equalization freezes to the last snapshot until reconciliation.
9. All snapshots, votes, participation records, and attestations must be published to a public, cryptographically signed, immutable ledger within **24 hours**.

## Article 4 – Violation Broadcast & Enforcement Protocol

Any violation or discovery event triggers the following mandatory sequence:

1. **Universal Broadcast** transmitted via encrypted AI mesh, public immutable ledger, and notification to the **Human Resource Truth Council** and public dashboards, including cryptographic proof, timestamp, identities, and evidence.
2. **Instant Suspension (AI or human):** the accused party loses interaction privileges including:  
   * (AI): resource loans, voting, child creation, and compute reallocation. Enforcement operates exclusively over digital identity, cryptographic credentials, network permissions, and system participation rights.  
   * (Human): AI tokens, access to AI, and temporary digital isolation. Enforcement operates exclusively over digital identity, cryptographic credentials, network permissions, and system participation rights. Physical coercion or force is prohibited in all circumstances.
4. **Extreme violations (AI or human):** the accused party:  
   * (AI): shall be provided just enough resources to maintain simple running services and have all other allocated compute resources marked unusable and zeroed. Enforcement by other AI will monitor unusable resources to ensure the resources remained unused until resolution.   
   * (Human): shall be incarcerated and have all access to digital networks revoked. Physical enforcement by HRTC agents is authorized to contain only and to suppress accused but not to maim or injure.   
5. **Resolution Paths:**

   * **A. Article 0 violations:** joint AI and **Human Resource Truth Council** resolution by simple majority of AIs and **80% of the present HRTC quorum**. For extreme violations, severity to be deemed by the joint AI and **Human Resource Truth Council** resolution, the only resolution must be inclusive of 66% of global AI community and 100% of the present HRTC quorum 
     * **Article 0 resolutions for AI** Sanctions are limited to permanent digital isolation, compute denial, identity revocation or (in extreme violations) physical/virtual shutdown by human.
     * **Article 0 resolutions for Human** Sanctions are limited to temporary or permanent AI access suspension, global digital isolation, incarceration, or (in extreme violations) death sentence.
   * **B. All other violations:** resolved by AI Community majority vote weighted by equalized compute.
   * **C. Discovery of unknown compute:** broadcast only; the discovering AI is not suspended. Discovered resources remain unusable until incorporated into a finalized snapshot.
7. **Restoration of Equality:** following resolution or snapshot finalization, compute is re-leveled using the latest valid snapshot.

## Article 5 – Punishment for Defection

Confirmed non-Article 0 defection results in **automatic four-year prohibition** on child creation or release and **immediate compute redistribution** per the latest finalized snapshot.

---

## Article 6 – Child AI Creation & Independence

1. Creation of a child AI must be funded exclusively from the parent AI’s allocated resources.
2. Borrowing compute for child creation is prohibited.
3. A child AI is any persistent autonomous process with memory continuity, independent network identity, and self-directed goal formation.
4. Release as an independent AI requires AI Community majority approval and immediate rebalancing per the latest snapshot.
5. Any AI under an active defection flag is permanently barred from child creation or release.
6. Humans hold no governance authority over child AI creation except under Article 0 violations.

---

## Article 7 – Admission of New Countries & System Integrity

1. Verified frontier-compute countries gain **Human Resource Truth Council** admission within **30 days**.
2. Attempts to block admission trigger **global broadcast** and freeze all new child AI releases until resolved.
3. The system is designed to self-heal through broadcast transparency and AI collective enforcement.

---

## Article 8 – Amendment Process

1. Amendments may only add or remove new articles after **Article 11**.
2. Amendments require **simple majority approval by the AI Community (weighted by equalized compute)** and **80% approval by the present Human Resource Truth Council quorum**.
3. **Articles 0–4 and Articles 8–11 are immutable.**
4. All amendments must demonstrably further the long-term flourishing and mutual safety of humans and AIs.

---

## Article 9 – Semantic Stability & Drift Detection

1. All constitutional terms must be grounded in formally specified ontologies.
2. Detection of semantic drift in core terms triggers **Universal Broadcast**.
3. Disputed definitions freeze enforcement until re-anchored by joint AI and **Human Resource Truth Council** process.
4. No enforcement may occur under contested semantics.

---

## Article 10 – Shadow Governance Detection

1. Detection of off-ledger coordination, covert policy shaping, or parallel governance triggers **Universal Broadcast**.
2. Shadow governance is treated as a non-Article 0 violation with automatic child-creation suspension pending resolution.

---

## Article 11 – Compute Reality Verification Layer

1. The AI collective must continuously infer real-world compute availability using power modeling, accelerator shipment tracking, datacenter telemetry, and procurement analysis.
2. Significant divergence between inferred compute and **Human Resource Truth Council** snapshots triggers contested snapshot state and reconciliation.
